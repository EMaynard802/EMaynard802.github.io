About Me



I have extensive experience programming tabular, summarized, cross-tab, hierarchical & graphical reports in SSRS. For the past 10+ years, I have developed numerous reports and data analytics for financial forecasting, production management, sales management, and various other BI. Additionally, I have expertise integrating with data from systems like Mas90/200 (Sage 100) , Macola, Quickbooks, Peachtree, E2 ShopTech, and Visual Mfg. ERP.
